The SWEEP

The hero took the treasure. You get the cleanup shift.

The SWEEP is a first-person, turn-based dungeon blobber where you play Unit 7, a sanitation contractor sent to clean a dungeon after the big heroic run. Traps are still live, hostile remnants still patrol, and your KPI is simple: 100% Clean per floor.

Key Features

  • First-person, grid-based movement with classic cardinal turns
  • Turn-based actions with spatial puzzle pressure
  • Three-slot inventory that forces tradeoffs between tools and debris
  • Cleanup-driven objective: haul debris to disposal chutes for clean percentage
  • Five handcrafted floors with escalating hazards

How It Plays

  • Move tile-by-tile through hostile cleanup zones
  • Use the right tool against the right hazard type
  • Pick up neutralized debris manually
  • Route debris to disposal chutes to raise your floor score
  • Manage HP, positioning, and inventory under constant turn pressure

Controls (Keyboard)

  • W, A, S, D: Move
  • Q, E: Turn left/right
  • 1, 2, 3: Use utility slot
  • Space: Pick up
  • F: Analyze target

Made With
